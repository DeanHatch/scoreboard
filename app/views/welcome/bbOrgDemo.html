Basketball Organizer Demo

Setup of a League or Tournament involves determining the format of the Competition (either a League or Season with or without Playoffs or a Tournament with or without Pool Play) and how the Competition will be structured (Divisions, Conferences, Age Groups, or whatever.)  Additionally, Dates, Locations, Groups, and Teams need to be determined.

Setup can be done all at one go or can be done in bits and pieces.  If you leave Setup and come back, everything will be as you left it.

Once Setup has been done, scheduling becomes a matter of choosing from the choices you have made available in the Setup. Round-Robin scheduling and Single-Elimination brackets setup help is available but you always have the ability to schedule what you want on your own. The Admin page includes a link to the Scheduler page.

Setup for Basketball is a tad different than for other sports. For example, Soccer has draws, basketball does not. We'll manage these details for you.
