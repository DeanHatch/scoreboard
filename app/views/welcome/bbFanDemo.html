Basketball Team and Fan Demo

The Public View

Users view standings, scores, and schedules through pages which are easy to navigate. When you follow the link to the Demo, below, group names appear on the right. Click on a group name to show the Standings for teams in that group. Or click on the link for Scores or Schedules next to the group name to get the scores or the scheduled games for teams in that group. If you click on a team name, you get everything for that team. Go ahead, click on the Demo link below and give it a try! You can do it!

The idea is for teams and their fans to be able to see what they want.

As a bonus, at the bottom of an individual team's Standings, Scores and Schedule page is a link which goes to a page that allows people to sign up to get email or text alerts whenever that team's schedule changes or whenever a final score is entered for that team.
